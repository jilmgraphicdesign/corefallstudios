System Destroyed

The Utopia Project has been destroyed. You stare at your screen as reality around you begins to glitch and delete. Your computer is the only remaining constant in a world of collapsing reality. In a flash, you feel yourself being pulled forward into the machine. A riptide of static tears you apart down to the smallest molecule and rearranges you back together again. When you blink your eyes open, you find yourself in a digital landscape of neon lines. Utopia is gone, all that remains is Corefall.

Someone will surely pay for this, but it won't be you. As far as anyone else is concerned Arne Kolbeck just initiated that program. Hope there aren't any unseen consequences to your actions.
