The World of Corefall

On the surface, neon hubs hum with life where you trade, form guilds, and duel. A cyber fantasy skyline of glowing wireframes rises into vaporwave spires and arenas that never sleep.

Beneath the surface, a corrupted labyrinth invites raids against shifting anomalies. The Worm twists geometry into jagged neon and warped voids, layering in glitch stingers and unsettling audio for high-risk, high-reward runs.

The Core is calling. Stand with the Aegisflame, strike with the Underwatch, or bend the grid with the Ciphers and push the world toward order, force, or chaos.

⌄

Two Layers, One Living World

  • Surface Hubs: Social cities for crafting, markets, and faction outreach. Join guilds, trade cosmetic sets, queue for arenas.
  • Under-Tunnels: Instanced dungeons and raids with scaling difficulty, timed challenges, and rare drops influenced by world events.
  • Dynamic Influence: Faction wins, guild projects, and player choices nudge the world unlocking events, vendors, and routes.

Order of the Aegisflame

Guardians of the Core

  • Defensive ops, purification rituals, barrier construction.
  • Ideal classes: Healers, Engineers.
  • Rewards: Sacred artifacts, construction materials.

The Underwatch Legion

Overwhelming force

  • Assault ops, covert raids, brutal PvP.
  • Ideal classes: Berserkers, Sentinels.
  • Rewards: Tactical gear, offensive skill unlocks.

The Ciphers

Embrace the Worm

  • Infiltration, corruption spreads, data manipulation.
  • Ideal classes: Assassins, Psychics.
  • Rewards: Hacked abilities, forbidden lore.
