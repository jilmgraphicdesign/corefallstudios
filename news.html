CSO Statement

March 25, 2018 — After stepping into the Chief Security Officer role, Arne Kolbeck shared: “I’ve been a Corefall player since the early alphas, so joining the team feels a bit like coming home. I’m grateful for the chance to help care for a world I love.”

“My focus is clear: keep players safe, keep your data private, and protect the integrity of the game. That means stronger account protections, privacy-first practices, and fair-play systems that deter cheats and respond quickly to exploits and coordinated attacks. We’ll communicate openly about issues and fixes, and we’ll keep things stable so you can jump in with confidence.”
